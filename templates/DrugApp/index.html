An analysis of the U.S. Opioid Epidemic

U.S. Opioid Prescriptions

The opioid crisis is devastating communities across America. The latest statistics indicate that the total yearly overdose deaths due to opioids reached a new high of 70,237 in 2017. In October 2017, President Trump declared the opioid crisis a public health emergency.

Currently no system exists that allows government officials and prescribers to simply view the volume of prescriptions that are taking place overall. So we have created this site to help adress that problem. We have a database of information about prescribers and drugs. The data are sorted and accessable so that all parties can freely analyze the data themselves and seek understanding. Our hope is that through this understanding we can all work to make the world a better place!
